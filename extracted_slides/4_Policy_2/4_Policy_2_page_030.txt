Updating the value network

• Recall $v(s_t; \boldsymbol{w})$ is an approximation to $V_\pi(s_t) = \mathbb{E}[U_t \vert s_t]$.

• Prediction error: $\delta_t = v(s_t; \boldsymbol{w}) - u_t$.

• Gradient: $\frac{\partial \delta_t^2/2}{\partial \boldsymbol{w}} = \delta_t \cdot \frac{\partial v(s_t; \boldsymbol{w})}{\partial \boldsymbol{w}}$.

• Gradient descent:
    $$\boldsymbol{w} \leftarrow \boldsymbol{w} - \alpha \cdot \delta_t \cdot \frac{\partial v(s_t; \boldsymbol{w})}{\partial \boldsymbol{w}}.$$
