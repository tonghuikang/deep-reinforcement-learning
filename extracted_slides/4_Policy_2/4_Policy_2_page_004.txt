Value Functions

• Discounted return:
    $U_t = R_t + \gamma \cdot R_{t+1} + \gamma^2 \cdot R_{t+2} + \gamma^3 \cdot R_{t+3} + \cdots$

• Action-value function:
    $Q_\pi(s_t, a_t) = \mathbb{E}[U_t \vert s_t, a_t]$.

• State-value function:
    $V_\pi(s_t) = \mathbb{E}_A[Q_\pi(s_t, A) \vert s_t]$.
