Single-Agent Policy Gradient for MARL

• The $1^{st}$ agent solves: $\max_{\boldsymbol{\theta}^1} J^1(\boldsymbol{\theta}^1, \boldsymbol{\theta}^2, \ldots, \boldsymbol{\theta}^n)$.
• The $2^{nd}$ agent solves: $\max_{\boldsymbol{\theta}^2} J^2(\boldsymbol{\theta}^1, \boldsymbol{\theta}^2, \ldots, \boldsymbol{\theta}^n)$.
$\vdots$
• The $n^{th}$ agent solves: $\max_{\boldsymbol{\theta}^n} J^n(\boldsymbol{\theta}^1, \boldsymbol{\theta}^2, \ldots, \boldsymbol{\theta}^n)$.
