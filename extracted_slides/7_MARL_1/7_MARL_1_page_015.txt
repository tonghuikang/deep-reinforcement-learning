Returns

• Let $R^i_t$ be the reward received by the $i$-th agent at time $t$.

• Return (of the $i$-th agent):
    $U^i_t = R^i_t + R^i_{t+1} + R^i_{t+2} + R^i_{t+3} + \cdots$

• Discounted return (of the $i$-th agent):
    $U^i_t = R^i_t + \gamma \cdot R^i_{t+1} + \gamma^2 \cdot R^i_{t+2} + \gamma^3 \cdot R^i_{t+3} + \cdots$
    
    Here, $\gamma \in [0, 1]$ is the discount rate.
