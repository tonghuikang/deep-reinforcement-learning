Terminology: policy

                                  policy π

[Game screenshot showing Mario platformer level with labeled actions: up arrow, left arrow, right arrow indicating possible actions]

• π(a | s) is the probability of taking action A = a given state s, e.g.,
  • π(left | s) = 0.2,
  • π(right| s) = 0.1,
  • π(up | s) = 0.7.

• Upon observing state S = s, the agent's action A can be random.
