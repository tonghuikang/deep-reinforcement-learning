Action-Value Function

Definition: Discounted return.

• $U_t = R_t + \gamma \cdot R_{t+1} + \gamma^2 \cdot R_{t+2} + \gamma^3 \cdot R_{t+3} + \cdots$

Definition: Action-value function.

• $Q_\pi(s_t, a_t) = \mathbb{E}[U_t \vert S_t = s_t, A_t = a_t]$.

The expectation is taken w.r.t.
actions $A_{t+1}, A_{t+2}, A_{t+3}, \cdots$
and states $S_{t+1}, S_{t+2}, S_{t+3}, \cdots$
