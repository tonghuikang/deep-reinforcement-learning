Policy Gradient

Policy gradient: $\frac{\partial V_\pi(s)}{\partial \theta} = \mathbb{E}_A \left[\frac{\partial \ln \pi(A \vert s;\theta)}{\partial \theta} Q_\pi(s, A)\right]$.

• Recall that $f(s, a; \theta) = \ln \pi(a \vert s; \theta) + \text{const}$.

• Thus the policy gradient is equal to:
    
    $\frac{\partial V_\pi(s)}{\partial \theta} = \mathbb{E}_A \left[\frac{\partial f(s,A;\theta)}{\partial \theta} \cdot Q_\pi(s, A)\right]$.
