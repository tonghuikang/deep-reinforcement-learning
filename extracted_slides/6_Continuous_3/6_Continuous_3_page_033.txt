Value Functions

Definition: Discounted return.

• $U_t = R_t + \gamma \cdot R_{t+1} + \gamma^2 \cdot R_{t+2} + \gamma^3 \cdot R_{t+3} + \cdots$

Definition: Action-value function.

• $Q_\pi(s, a) = \mathbb{E} [U_t \vert S_t = s, A_t = a]$.

Definition: State-value function.

• $V_\pi(s) = \mathbb{E}_A [Q_\pi(s, A)]$.

• The expectation is taken w.r.t. $A \sim \pi(\cdot \vert s; \theta)$.
