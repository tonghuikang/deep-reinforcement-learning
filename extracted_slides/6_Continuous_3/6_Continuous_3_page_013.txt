Continuous Control

• Observe state $s$.

• Compute mean and log variance using the neural network:

    $\hat{\boldsymbol{\mu}} = \boldsymbol{\mu}(s; \boldsymbol{\theta}^{\mu})$ and $\hat{\boldsymbol{\rho}} = \boldsymbol{\rho}(s; \boldsymbol{\theta}^{\rho})$.

• Compute $\hat{\sigma}_i^2 = \exp(\hat{\rho}_i)$, for all $i = 1, \cdots, d$.

• Randomly sample action $\mathbf{a}$ by

    $a_i \sim N(\hat{\mu}_i, \hat{\sigma}_i^2)$, for all $i = 1, \cdots, d$.
