Centralized Training

Critic 1        Central Controller        Critic n
                    {a^i, o^i, r^i}_{i=1}^n

• The central controller trains the critics, q(o, a; w^i), for all i.
• To update w^i, TD algorithm takes as inputs:
  • All the actions: a = [a^1, a^2, \cdots, a^n].
  • All the observations: o = [o^1, o^2, \cdots, o^n].
  • The i-th reward: r^i.
