Choice 1: $\mathbf{b}=0$

**Policy gradient:**
$\frac{\partial V_\pi(s_t)}{\partial \boldsymbol{\theta}} = \mathbb{E}_{A_t \sim \pi} \left[\frac{\partial \ln \pi(A_t \vert s_t; \boldsymbol{\theta})}{\partial \boldsymbol{\theta}} \cdot (Q_\pi(s_t, A_t) - b)\right]$.

• We can simply set $b = 0$.
• It becomes the standard policy gradient:
    $\frac{\partial V_\pi(s_t)}{\partial \boldsymbol{\theta}} = \mathbb{E}_{A_t \sim \pi} \left[\frac{\partial \ln \pi(A_t \vert s_t; \boldsymbol{\theta})}{\partial \boldsymbol{\theta}} \cdot Q_\pi(s_t, A_t)\right]$.
