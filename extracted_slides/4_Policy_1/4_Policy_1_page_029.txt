Stochastic Policy Gradient

**Stochastic policy gradient:**
$\mathbf{g}(a_t) = \frac{\partial \ln \pi(a_t\vert s_t;\boldsymbol{\theta})}{\partial \boldsymbol{\theta}} \cdot (Q_\pi(s_t, a_t) - b)$.
